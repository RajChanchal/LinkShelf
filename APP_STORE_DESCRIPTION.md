# App Store Description - Updated

## Updated Description (Recommended)

LinkShelf is a simple macOS menu bar app that lets you store frequently used links and copy them instantly with a single click.

If you often need to share your LinkedIn profile, GitHub profile, portfolio, resume, or any recurring link, LinkShelf removes the friction. No more opening websites, navigating to profiles, or copying URLs from the address bar.

Just click the menu bar icon, tap the link, and it's copied.

---

## Key Features

• **Menu bar access** - Instant availability from your menu bar
• **One-click copy** - Copy any link to clipboard instantly
• **Visual identification** - Automatic favicon display for easy recognition
• **Quick search** - Find links instantly with real-time search
• **Open in browser** - Optional secondary action to open links
• **Full management** - Add, edit, delete, and reorder links
• **Clean interface** - Distraction-free, native macOS design
• **Local storage** - All data stored securely on your Mac, no account required
• **Multi-language** - Supports 10 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese, and more
• **Fast & lightweight** - No bloat, just works

---

## Perfect for

• Developers and engineers sharing GitHub, LinkedIn, portfolio links
• Designers and freelancers with frequently accessed resources
• Recruiters and job seekers managing multiple profile links
• Students and professionals organizing important links
• Anyone who reuses links daily

---

## Privacy First

• All data stored locally on your device
• No tracking or analytics
• No data sent to external servers
• Your links remain private

---

LinkShelf is intentionally minimal. It focuses on speed, privacy, and convenience so you can stay in flow and get back to what matters.
