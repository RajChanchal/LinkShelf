# Privacy Policy for LinkShelf

**Last Updated:** December 2024  
**Effective Date:** December 2024

---

## Introduction

LinkShelf ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use LinkShelf, a macOS menu bar application.

By using LinkShelf, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

LinkShelf stores the following information **locally on your Mac**:

- **Link Data**: Titles and URLs of links you add to the app
- **Link Order**: The order in which you arrange your links
- **App Preferences**: Basic app state information (e.g., whether the app has launched before)

### Data We Do NOT Collect

LinkShelf does **NOT** collect, transmit, or store:

- ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- ❌ Usage analytics or tracking data
- ❌ Device information or identifiers
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Browsing history or web activity
- ❌ Any data transmitted to external servers

---

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Storage Only

All information stored by LinkShelf is kept **exclusively on your device**:

- **Link Data**: Used to display your saved links in the app interface
- **Link Order**: Used to maintain your preferred link arrangement
- **App Preferences**: Used to provide a better user experience

### No External Transmission

- **No data is sent to external servers**
- **No data is shared with third parties**
- **No analytics or tracking services are used**
- **No cloud synchronization** (data remains on your device only)

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location

- All data is stored locally on your Mac using macOS **UserDefaults**
- Data is stored in the app's sandboxed container
- Data is encrypted by macOS system-level encryption

### Security Measures

- **App Sandbox**: LinkShelf runs in a sandboxed environment, limiting access to your system
- **Local-Only Storage**: No network transmission of your data
- **System Encryption**: Your data benefits from macOS's built-in encryption

### Data Retention

- Your link data remains on your device until you:
  - Delete individual links
  - Delete the app (which removes all associated data)
- We do not retain any copies of your data

---

## Third-Party Services

LinkShelf does **NOT** use any third-party services that collect or process your data, including:

- ❌ Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
- ❌ Advertising networks
- ❌ Cloud storage services
- ❌ Crash reporting services
- ❌ User tracking services

### Network Access

LinkShelf only accesses the network when you explicitly choose to:
- **Open a link in your browser** - This uses your default browser and standard macOS network APIs
- No data about which links you open is collected or transmitted

---

## Your Rights and Control

### Access to Your Data

- All your data is stored locally and accessible through the app interface
- You can view, edit, or delete any link at any time
- You have full control over all data stored by the app

### Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

1. **Deleting individual links**: Use the delete option in the app
2. **Deleting all data**: Uninstall the app, which removes all associated data from your Mac

### Data Export

Currently, LinkShelf does not provide a built-in export feature. All data remains on your device. Export functionality may be added in future versions.

---
